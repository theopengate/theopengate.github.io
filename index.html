About Us


A vibrant Christian community located in the Westhills area of Langford. We are proudly apart of The Anglican Network in Canada and we offer both a traditional and contemporary service of worship rooted in the Anglican tradition. More than that, we offer a welcoming environment for people from all walks of life to come closer to God.
