About Us


A vibrant Christian community located in the Westhills area of Langford. We are proudly apart of The Anglican Network in Canada and we offer both a traditional and contemporary service of worship rooted in the Anglican tradition. More than that, we offer a welcoming environment for people from all walks of life to come closer to God.

Designer Templates

We’ve crafted some handsome templates for you to use. Go ahead and click 'Continue to layouts' to browse through them. You can easily go back to edit your page before publishing. Creating pages manually

If you prefer to not use the automatic generator, push a branch named gh-pages to your repository to create a page manually. In addition to supporting regular HTML content, GitHub Pages support Jekyll, a simple, blog aware static site generator. Jekyll makes it easy to create site-wide headers and footers without having to copy them across every page. It also offers intelligent blog support and other advanced templating features.

Authors and Contributors
